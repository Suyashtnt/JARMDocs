Ruby Ore
========



About
-----



Ruby Ore is a naturally generating ore found in all biomes in the overworld 
and the ruby dimension. It drops 1 :doc:`ruby <../Items/ruby>` when mined with a pickaxe with a harvest level of 2(iron)
